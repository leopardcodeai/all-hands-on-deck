## Description (4000 chars max)

Everyone sees the frame before the photo is taken.

All Hands on Deck turns one iPhone into a live viewfinder that streams to every phone in the group — natively over Multipeer, or in a browser without installing anything. No more mystery photos. No more retakes because someone blinked.

**How it works**
One person — the Captain — opens the app, points the camera, and starts a session. Everyone else joins by scanning the QR code or tapping a link. In seconds the whole crew sees exactly what the camera sees, live at 3 fps.

**Smart capture**
- Live hints tell you when someone's drifting out of frame ("Group too far left", "Someone's getting cut off")
- Best-Shot Burst fires 5 frames and lets AI rank them by face count, open eyes, and sharpness — the Captain picks the winner
- A precise countdown syncs across every device simultaneously

**Apple Watch remote**
Start the timer, fire immediately, or cancel a countdown — all from your wrist. The Watch shows a live countdown so you can hold the pose until the very last second.

**Web viewers, no app required**
Anyone can join from Safari or Chrome on any device. They get the live preview, the countdown, and the final photo — no download needed.

**Privacy first**
- No accounts, no sign-up
- No data collected or stored anywhere
- Photos stay on your device until you explicitly tap Save
- Sessions expire automatically after 10 minutes of idle time — nothing lingers

## Notes for Review Team

This app uses the camera exclusively for live preview streaming to nearby devices.
It uses Multipeer Connectivity and Local Network access to discover and connect
to other iPhones on the same Wi-Fi or via Bluetooth. No data leaves the device
except the live preview frames, which are transmitted directly to other users in
the same session and discarded immediately after.

The app does not use the camera in the background and does not store any
video or photo data without an explicit "Save" action by the user.
